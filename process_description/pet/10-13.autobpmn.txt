The process begins with an initial step where a request is initiated. Specifically, an entity referred to as INQ sends out a request for transaction data. Once this request is transmitted, it is received by another entity known as IP. The next step involves IP reviewing and checking the request that was sent by INQ. After thoroughly examining the request, IP proceeds to respond to the inquiry made by INQ. The process concludes once IP has provided an answer to the question posed by INQ.
