The process begins with an employee searching for the police report. Once the police report is found, the employee combines it with other necessary documents to create a complete set of claim documentation. After assembling the documentation, a claims handler takes over to calculate an initial estimate for the claim. Following this, the claims handler develops an action plan based on the initial estimate. The process then moves to a claims manager, who negotiates a settlement based on the claim estimate. After reaching a settlement, the claims manager communicates the outcome to the claimant. The process concludes once the claimant has been informed of the result.
