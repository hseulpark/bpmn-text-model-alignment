The process begins with an initial action where the MSPN sends a dismissal. Following this, the MSPO takes the next step by reviewing the dismissal that was sent by the MSPN. After the review, a decision point is reached where the MSPO must choose between two possible outcomes.

In the first possible outcome, the MSPO decides to reject the dismissal of the MSPN. If this decision is made, the process continues to the next step, which is another decision point.

In the second possible outcome, the MSPO confirms the dismissal of the MSPN. If this decision is made, the process also continues to the same subsequent decision point.

Regardless of whether the MSPO rejects or confirms the dismissal, the process converges at this decision point, and from there, it proceeds to the conclusion of the process. The process then ends.
