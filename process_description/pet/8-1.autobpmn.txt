The process begins when the functional department makes a request to fill a vacant position. Once this demand is made, the next step is to advertise the post to attract potential candidates. Following the advertisement, applicants submit their applications for the position. After receiving the applications, the next step involves reviewing and checking these applications to assess the suitability of the candidates. Once the applications have been thoroughly checked, the process proceeds to filling the post with the most suitable candidate. The process concludes after the position has been successfully filled.
