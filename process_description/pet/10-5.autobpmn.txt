The process begins with the MPON initiating a request for a device takeover bid from the MPOO. Following this request, the MPOO responds by sending a tender for the equipment takeover back to the MPON. Once the MPON receives the tender, they proceed to place an order with the MPOO. After receiving the order, the MPOO confirms it and sends the necessary master data to the MPON. This confirmation and data transfer mark the end of the process.
